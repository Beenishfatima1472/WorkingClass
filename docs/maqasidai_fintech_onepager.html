How to engage

Three ways to work with MaqasidAI

Whether you want a one-time audit report for your board or ongoing compliance monitoring embedded in your pipeline, we have a tier for it.

Tier 01
Shadow Audit
Submit your AI system's outputs. We run the full 50-point MACI evaluation and deliver a signed audit report — no access to your model required.
Deliverable: Full MACI report · Cert. status · Findings summary
Tier 02 — Most requested
Integration Audit
Embed the MACI scanner at your inference output layer. We audit in real-time — no changes to your model, stack, or inference pipeline.

Certification Partnership
